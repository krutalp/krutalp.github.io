Breast Cancer Histopathology Classification Project.

Project poster: Click Me.



Description:

Breast Cancer Histopathology Database (BreakHis), developed by the UFPR, is comprised of over 9,000 histopathological images of Breast Cancer Tissue collected from over 80 patients. In this project, we extract histopathological images of only 40x magnification. We developed a scalable, to-production, classification algorithm to predict tumor type (benign or malignant) of the image.
